MAKEUP PORTFOLIO

#Design   #Camouflage   #Photography

Cosmetics are used to enhance your appearance. Makeup back in the day was just simple eye coloring or some material for the body. Nowadays makeup plays an important role for both men and women and that is why the makeup industry is changing in various ways for the better as there are a lot of products to help achieve what consumers are in need of. Consumers are on a journey of exploration enjoying new products and finding brands. I have put together this portfolio in a creative way to show everyone my work on all my clients so far, depending on what skin type they have and what products will suit their skin in the best way.

Down below are some images showing before and after of some of my clients I have worked with in the past, including what makeup product I've used and why I've chosen them.

Client 1:

I used an eyeshadow brush to evenly blend-out the colour on her whole eyelid giving her a natural mature look, concealed under her eyes to get rid of her dark circles, a blush brush to give colour to her cheeks; I also used a warm-pink lip colour to brighten her lips with a disposable lip brush.

Client 2:

I used the AABT eyeshadow brush to evenly blend the colours to create a soft effect in her crease with a golden effect on the inner corner of her eye, concealed under her eyes with a concealer brush. I used the AABT blush brush to enlighten her cheeks with colour; a dark-nude colour on her lips to assist the 'night' vibe with a disposable lip brush.

Client 3:

I used a light-brown eyeshadow colour to complement her deepset eyes, I lightly concealed under her eyes as she didn't need much concealer. I applied the pink blush with an AABT blush brush to emphasise the apples of her cheek, and a pink lip colour to give colour to her lips with a disposable lip brush
